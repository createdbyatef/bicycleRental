The Right Bike Store, owned and managed by Groodies Enterprise, is an external company established around 2013. For its branch located at Kolej Kediaman Tun Fatimah, The Right Bike Store provides bicycle rental services to facilitate the convenience of UTHM students on an hourly or weekly basis. Since 2019, the company has been collaborating with UTHM to provide this service exclusively for students.

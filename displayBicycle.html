UTHM Map

The map of Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) helps students and visitors navigate the campus easily. It shows important areas like faculty buildings, the library, residential colleges, and sports facilities. Key landmarks include the Masjid Sultan Ibrahim, the library, and various hostels such as Kolej Tun Dr. Ismail and Kolej Tun Fatimah.
